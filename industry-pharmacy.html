What makes pricing hard in pharmacy & cosmetics

Regulatory accuracy

Pharmacy pricing is audited. A single mismatched tag is a compliance exposure, not just a customer-service issue.

High-density displays

Cosmetics and pharmacy peg hooks pack many small SKUs into little space, where standard tags are unreadable.

Frequent reformulation

Product variants and batch changes mean constant label churn that paper can’t keep up with.
