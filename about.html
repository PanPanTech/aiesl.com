// OUR STORY

R&D, manufacturing and sales under one roof

PanPanTech provides wireless smart devices and IoT technology services built around electronic shelf label systems. With a customer-centric approach, the company combines software, hardware and cloud services to help clients reduce operating costs, optimize workflows and improve collaboration efficiency.

Our solutions serve retail, electronics, baking, apparel, warehousing and logistics, fresh produce and chain supermarket customers in more than 30 countries and regions worldwide.

Innovation remains the key to our development. A professional R&D team continues to explore new application fields, while a 3,200 sq m ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing facility supports standardized production with high-speed SMT lines, soldering lines and automated testing equipment.

// MILESTONES

PanPanTech company timeline

2015-2024

OEM manufacturing and export trade foundation

Before 2024, PanPanTech focused on OEM processing and international trade, building practical experience in product manufacturing, supply chain coordination and overseas customer service.

2024.08

Solution strategy upgraded around ESL and IoT

From August 2024, the company began presenting its electronic shelf label capabilities as a more complete solution, connecting ESL hardware, wireless smart devices and cloud-based service workflows for retail customers.

2024.10

Industry scenario materials standardized

Marketing and solution materials were organized around real customer scenarios including retail, electronics, baking, apparel, warehousing and logistics, fresh produce and chain supermarkets.

2025.03

Software, hardware and cloud collaboration strengthened

The team refined the customer-centric service model, combining device deployment, system integration and cloud services to help clients reduce operating costs and optimize workflows.

2025.08

Manufacturing and quality capability reinforced

The 3,200 sq m manufacturing facility, ISO 9001:2015 quality system, high-speed SMT lines, soldering lines and automated testing equipment support standardized production and reliable delivery.

2026

Global customer base across 30+ countries

PanPanTech continues to serve customers in more than 30 countries and regions, expanding ESL and IoT applications through ongoing R&D and industry-focused delivery.
