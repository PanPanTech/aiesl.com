// OUR STORY

From a price tag to a pricing platform

AiESL was founded in 2015 with a single conviction: the shelf edge is the most underused surface in retail. What began as an electronic shelf label manufacturer has grown into a full AI pricing platform — combining hardware, open middleware and a decision engine.

Today we operate research, manufacturing and sales under one roof, with an R&D-led team and a production base running ten SMT lines. Our labels and platform serve supermarkets, pharmacies, electronics retail and warehouses across more than 30 countries.

We are part of PanPanTech, and also operate the AiEinkSmart and Pyroglaux brands serving global retail technology markets.
