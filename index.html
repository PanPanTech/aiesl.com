// FAQ

Common questions about AI ESL

Still unsure? Our retail solution engineers answer questions directly — no sales script.

An AI ESL is an electronic shelf label whose pricing and content are driven by an AI decision engine. Instead of only mirroring the POS price, an AI ESL system analyzes demand, inventory and competitor data to recommend or automatically apply dynamic prices at the shelf edge in real time.

AiESL electronic shelf labels use ultra-low-power e-paper displays that consume energy only during updates. A typical label runs 7 to 10 years on a single battery under standard 3-changes-per-day operation.

Absolutely. AiESL's open-platform middleware runs on standard MQTT protocols, seamlessly supporting mixed-vendor environments across 23+ major hardware brands. You can keep your existing labels on standard shelves and deploy our high-resolution 2.66" or 2.9" labels only on promotional zones or hooks — cutting your total migration and upgrade costs by over 50%.

Approved price changes propagate to every connected label in under one second. A chain can update thousands of SKUs across all locations simultaneously, eliminating the gap between register and shelf pricing.

AiESL is deployed across supermarkets, convenience stores, electronics retail, pharmacies, warehouses and food service. Label sizes from 1.5" to 13.3" cover everything from small shelf tags to large promotional displays.
