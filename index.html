Common questions about AI ESL

An AI ESL is an electronic shelf label whose pricing and content are driven by an AI decision engine. Instead of only mirroring the POS price, the system analyzes demand, inventory and competitor data to recommend or automatically apply dynamic prices at the shelf edge in real time.

Yes. AiESL's open-platform middleware runs on standard MQTT, supporting mixed-vendor environments across 5+ hardware brand integrations. Keep existing labels on standard shelves and deploy high-resolution AiESL labels only where they add value — cutting migration cost by over 50%.

AiESL labels use ultra-low-power e-paper that consumes energy only during updates. A typical label runs 7–10 years on a single battery under standard 3-changes-per-day operation.

Approved price changes propagate to every connected label in under one second. A chain can update thousands of SKUs across all locations simultaneously, eliminating the gap between register and shelf pricing.

AiESL is deployed across supermarkets, convenience stores, electronics retail, pharmacies, warehouses and food service. Label sizes from 1.5" to 13.3" cover everything from small shelf tags to large promotional displays.
