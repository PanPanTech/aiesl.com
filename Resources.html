Common questions about AI ESL

Still unsure? Our retail solution engineers answer questions directly — no sales script.

Talk to an engineer

An AI ESL is an electronic shelf label whose pricing and content are driven by an AI decision engine. Instead of only mirroring the POS price, the system analyzes demand, inventory and competitor data to recommend or automatically apply dynamic prices at the shelf edge in real time.

A regular ESL simply displays whatever price the POS sends. An AI ESL adds an intelligence layer that decides what the price should be — weighing demand, stock, margin targets and competitor signals — then updates the shelf automatically within rules you set.

Paper tags require manual re-tagging on every price change, create register-vs-shelf mismatches and consume paper and labor indefinitely. Electronic labels update store-wide in under a second, eliminate pricing errors, and pay back the one-time hardware cost through labor savings — typically within 1–2 years.

AiESL labels use ultra-low-power e-paper that consumes energy only during updates. A typical label runs 7–10 years on a single battery under standard 3-changes-per-day operation.
