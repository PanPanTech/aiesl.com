// THE CHALLENGE

What makes pricing hard in supermarket & grocery

Price integrity at scale

Tens of thousands of SKUs change weekly. Manual paper tags drift out of sync with the POS, creating checkout disputes and compliance risk.

Perishable waste

Static prices can’t respond to expiry windows or demand swings, so fresh stock is marked down too late — or not at all.

Promo execution

National promotions need to hit every aisle at open. Re-tagging overnight is slow, error-prone and expensive.
